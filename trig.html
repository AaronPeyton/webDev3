Trigonometry

Quiz Mode

1.what is the equation for sin(a+b)

sin(a)*cos(b) + sin(b)*cos(a)

2.what is the equation for cos(a-b)

cos(a)cos(b)+sin(a)sin(b)

3.what is the equation for cos(2a)

(cos(a))^2 - (sin(a))^2

4.what is the equation for cos(a/2)

+/- sqrt( (1+cos(a))/2 )

5.what is the equation for tan(a/2)

(1-cos(a))/sin(a)

6.what is the equation for law of sines

sin(a)/a = sin(b)/b = sin(c)/c
